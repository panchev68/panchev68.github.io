Detailed Description

Detects rising and falling edges in a sequence of data.

The FindPeaks class provides functionality to detect rising and falling edges in a sequence of integer samples using a sliding window and internal state. It is designed for embedded applications requiring efficient and deterministic edge detection, maintaining peak values and their positions for analysis.
