Подробно описание

Manager service class.

Manages a collection of ServiceBase-derived services, providing singleton access, addition, removal, and update functionality. Ensures only one instance exists and prevents duplicate service registration. Designed for embedded Digi modules, enabling centralized management and update of service objects.
