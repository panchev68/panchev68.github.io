Lib::Xbee::API::Service::Parameters::Data::SerialNumber

Detailed Description

Represents the serial number of the XBee device.

The SerialNumber union represents the serial number of the XBee device. It contains the low and high parts of the serial number, as well as the combined 64-bit serial number.
