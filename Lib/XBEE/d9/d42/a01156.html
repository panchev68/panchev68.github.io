Lib::Digi::API::Service::Parameters

Detailed Description

Manages and manipulates various parameters related to XBee devices.

The Parameters class is responsible for managing and manipulating various parameters related to XBee devices, including reading and writing static and dynamic parameters. It uses the Core::LocalAtCommand and Core::RemoteAtCommand classes to send and receive AT commands to/from the local and remote XBee modules.
