Lib::Digi::API::Service::ChannelScanner

Detailed Description

Scans channels and updates connection quality in the Xbee module.

The ChannelScanner class is responsible for scanning channels and updating connection quality in the Xbee module. It inherits from the ServiceBase class and utilizes FreeRTOS for task and queue management.
