Lib::Xbee::Core::Frame0x17::DestinationAddress

Detailed Description

A union to represent a 64-bit destination address.

This union allows the destination address to be accessed either as a 64-bit unsigned integer or as two 32-bit unsigned integers (most significant bits and least significant bits).
