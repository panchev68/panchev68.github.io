Detailed Description

Device driver manager for STM32 BSP.

Manages registration, initialization, and periodic tick invocation of device drivers in the STM32 BSP layer. Provides singleton access and ensures all registered drivers are initialized and periodically ticked.

Initializes all registered device drivers and starts periodic tick service.

Iterates through all registered device drivers, calls their init() method, and starts the TickService to periodically invoke the tick() method on each driver.
